ABOUT

'A festival for all the family'

Brooklyn Vibe Events Co. are proud to present the first Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival. We will be showing tweleve films over four nights at the spectacular Brooklyn Bridge Park between 5th & 8th Aug 2019.

BOFF19 is a festival for the whole family, bring your blankets and come relax in the open to watch some great films with an incredible backdrop. Tickets are FREE, thats right FREE! We only ask that you please register for tickets in advance.
